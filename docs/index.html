App to replace them all

Everything You Need to Run Your Studio

Counter replaces your spreadsheets, calendars, and sticky notes with one cohesive workspace.

👤

Client Management

Comprehensive profiles with contact details, health info, allergy notes, pronouns, and a full history of pieces, sessions, and spend.

🎨

Piece Tracking

Manage every tattoo from concept to healed. Track status, placement, size, pricing, tags, reference images, and artist ratings.

📅

Bookings & Sessions

Schedule consultations, sessions, and touch-ups. Auto-generated prep checklists, no-show tracking, and session-type pricing keep you organized.

💰

Financial Management

Record payments by method, track deposits and outstanding balances, apply discounts, and generate year-end financial reports as PDFs.

📷

Gallery & Image Library

Document work progress by stage. Show clients a curated public gallery while keeping sketches and stencils private. Build a personal inspiration library.

🔒

Privacy & Client Mode

Lock your business data behind Face ID, Touch ID, or a PIN. Hand your iPad to a client to browse the gallery without exposing sensitive info.

✉

Email Templates & Comms

Pre-built and custom email templates with smart tokens. Send from the app, attach images, and every communication is automatically logged.

📝

Agreements & Waivers

Consent forms, liability waivers, photo releases, and design approvals — all with digital signature capture and PDF export.

📈

PDF Reports

Generate professional client summaries, outstanding balance reports, finished-piece portfolios, and year-end financials at the tap of a button.

🕐

Availability Scheduling

Set weekly recurring slots, mark flash-only hours, and add overrides for holidays or special closures. Clients see when you're bookable.

⚙

Fully Customizable

Define custom session types, pricing tiers, discount categories, fee structures, and gallery groupings tailored to the way you work.

📱

Built for iPad

Native SwiftUI experience designed for the device you already use at your station. Fast, fluid, and works entirely offline.
