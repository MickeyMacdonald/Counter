# Counter

**Studio management built for tattoo artists, removal specialists, and hairdressers.**

Counter is a native iPad app that replaces your spreadsheets, calendars, and sticky notes with one cohesive workspace. Manage clients, bookings, sessions, finances, agreements, and your portfolio — all from the device you already use at your station.

## Features

- **Client Management** — Profiles with contact info, health notes, and full session/spend history
- **Piece Tracking** — Manage every tattoo from concept to healed with status, pricing, and reference images
- **Bookings & Sessions** — Schedule appointments with prep checklists, session-type pricing, and no-show tracking
- **Financial Management** — Payments, deposits, outstanding balances, discounts, and PDF year-end reports
- **Gallery & Image Library** — Client-facing gallery with private sketches/stencils and a personal inspiration library
- **Privacy & Client Mode** — Lock business data behind Face ID / Touch ID and hand your iPad to clients safely
- **Email Templates** — Smart-token templates with auto-logged communications
- **Agreements & Waivers** — Digital signature capture with PDF export
- **PDF Reports** — Client summaries, financials, portfolios, and flash inventories
- **Availability Scheduling** — Weekly slots, flash-only hours, and holiday overrides
- **Fully Customizable** — Custom session types, pricing tiers, discounts, fees, and gallery groups
- **100% Local & Private** — All data stays on your device
